Charter Regulations

Floating Adventures B.V. | Last updated: February 2026

1. General Rules

  • All charters are private and operated with licensed crew only.
  • By confirming a booking, guests agree to onboard safety instructions.
  • Routes and timings may change due to weather or safety conditions.

2. Booking and Payment

  • Reservations are confirmed after deposit or full payment (as agreed).
  • Remaining balance must be paid before departure unless stated otherwise.
  • Booking details must be accurate and provided on time.

3. Safety and Conduct

  • Guests must follow all captain/crew safety instructions at all times.
  • Unsafe behavior, damage, or severe intoxication may end the trip without refund.
  • Life jackets and safety equipment must be used when requested.

4. Weather and Cancellation

  • Trips run only in safe weather and sea conditions.
  • If conditions are unsafe, trips may be rescheduled or canceled.
  • Cancellation terms and any refund eligibility follow booking agreement timing.

5. Liability

Guests are responsible for personal belongings and for any damage caused by themselves or their party. The operator is not liable for incidents resulting from failure to follow safety instructions.
